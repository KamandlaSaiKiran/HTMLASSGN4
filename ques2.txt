A HTML entity is piece of text that begins with an ampersand and ends with a semicolon
  for exmaple, the entity for the copyright symbol is &copy.
  similarly,
     for less than symbol it is &lt;
     for ampersand symbol it is &amp;
     for greaterthan symbol it is &gt;
     for trade mark sign it is &trade;
     for registered sign it is &reg;
     for degree symbol it is &deg;

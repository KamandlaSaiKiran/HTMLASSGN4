   1.Navigation Tag <Nav>...</Nav>
       examples are: <nav>
      <a href="#">HTML</a> | 
      <a href="#">CSS</a> | 
      <a href="#">JavaScript</a> |

         examples are:
         <video width="400px" height="300px" controls>
         <source src=" movie.mp4 " type="video/mp4">
         </video>
